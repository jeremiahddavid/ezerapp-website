Introduction

EZer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Financial Data: Expense and income entries you manually add to the app
  • Goals Data: Savings goals and fund allocations you create
  • Budget Data: Budget categories and limits you set
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the EZer service
  • Display your financial data within the app
  • Generate analytics and insights about your spending
  • Sync your data across devices (if you enable cloud backup)
  • Send important service notifications

Data Storage & Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud backup:

  • Data is encrypted with AES-256 encryption before upload
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase)
  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • We never store your data unencrypted

What We Don't Do

  • We DO NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We DO NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We DO NOT access your bank accounts directly
  • We DO NOT store your bank passwords
  • We DO NOT read SMS messages (iOS) or store SMS content on servers

Your Rights (PDPA)

Under the Personal Data Protection Act, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ezerapp.com

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

Children's Privacy

EZer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@ezerapp.com

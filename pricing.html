Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No! You can start using EZer completely free without providing any payment information. The 30-day trial is available to everyone with no credit card required.

What happens after the 30-day trial?

After your trial ends, you'll need to subscribe (₹99/month for India) to continue using EZer. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends with no charges.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Is the ₹99/month price permanent for India?

Yes! The ₹99/month is our special pricing for users in India. This pricing is designed to make EZer accessible to everyone.

How does SMS auto-capture work?

On Android devices, EZer can read transaction SMS from your bank (with your permission) and automatically add them to your transaction list. SMS is processed locally on your device and never stored on our servers. Accuracy depends on your bank's SMS format, so we recommend reviewing auto-captured transactions.

Can I import my bank statements?

Yes! EZer supports importing PDF and CSV bank statements from most major Indian banks. The system automatically detects your bank's format and imports transactions. Manual review is recommended to ensure accuracy.

How many friends can I refer?

You can refer up to 10 friends. Each friend who actively uses EZer for 31 days earns you 31 days of free premium access, for a maximum of 310 days through referrals.

Is my financial data safe?

Absolutely! EZer uses bank-level AES-256 encryption for all your data. Your information is encrypted both at rest and in transit. We never have access to your bank account passwords or credentials. Read our Security page for more details.

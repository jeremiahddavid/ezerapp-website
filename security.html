Security Questions

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. All your financial data is encrypted with military-grade 256-bit AES encryption and stored locally on your device. We use the same encryption standards as banks and financial institutions.

Can EZer access my bank account?

No. EZer never asks for or stores your bank login credentials. We only read transaction SMS messages that your bank sends you, and this happens entirely on your device.

What happens to my SMS messages?

SMS messages are processed locally on your device to extract transaction information. The SMS content is never stored or uploaded to our servers. Only the extracted transaction details (amount, merchant, date) are saved.

How is cloud backup secured?

Cloud backups are completely optional. If you enable them, your data is encrypted with AES-256 before upload. The encryption key stays on your device, so even we cannot decrypt your backup.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You have complete control over your data. You can export all your data at any time and delete your account along with all associated data permanently.

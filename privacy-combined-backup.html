Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Android

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Version 1.0

Introduction

Welcome to EZer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the EZer mobile application on Android devices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Name (optional), Email address, Profile information
  • Currency and language preferences, Regional settings

Financial Data You Enter:

  • Transactions, Bank account names (not credentials)
  • Budget information, Savings goals, Bill information
  • Receipt images and scanned documents

Important: We NEVER ask for or store:

  • Bank account passwords or login credentials
  • Credit/debit card numbers, CVV/PIN codes
  • Social Security Numbers or Government ID numbers

1.2 Information Collected Automatically (Android-Specific)

SMS Messages (Android Only - Requires Your Permission):

When you grant SMS permission, EZer reads your SMS messages to automatically extract financial transaction information.

What We Do With SMS Data:

  • ✅ Process SMS locally on your device
  • ✅ Extract transaction details (amount, merchant, date)
  • ✅ Delete original SMS content immediately after processing
  • ❌ NEVER store complete SMS message text
  • ❌ NEVER upload SMS content to our servers
  • ❌ NEVER share SMS data with third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your financial transactions and balances
  • Categorize expenses and income automatically
  • Generate budgets and track spending
  • Monitor bill due dates and send reminders
  • Track savings goals and progress
  • Provide financial insights and analytics

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Local Storage (Android Device)

  • Encrypted Database: All financial data stored in encrypted SQLCipher database
  • AES-256 encryption standard
  • Encryption keys unique to your device and never leave your device
  • Data cannot be accessed without biometric authentication or PIN

4. Android-Specific Permissions

Permission Purpose Required?
READ_SMS Automatic transaction capture Optional
CAMERA Scan receipts Optional
USE_BIOMETRIC Secure app lock Optional
INTERNET Cloud backup, app updates Required

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✅ Access all your data stored in the app
  • ✅ Export your data (CSV, JSON, Excel, PDF formats)
  • ✅ Delete specific transactions or all data
  • ✅ Modify or correct any information
  • ✅ Withdraw consent for SMS access anytime

6. Data Sharing

❌ What We DON'T Do

  • • Store your SMS messages
  • • Sell your data to third parties
  • • Share data with advertisers
  • • Require bank passwords

✅ What We DO

  • • Encrypt all data with bank-level security
  • • Process SMS locally on device
  • • Give you complete data control
  • • Provide easy data export

7. Contact Us

Email: privacy@ezerapp.com

Support: support@ezerapp.com

Website: https://www.ezerapp.com/privacy

Response Time: Within 7 business days for privacy requests

Privacy Policy for iOS

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Version 1.0

Introduction

Welcome to EZer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the EZer mobile application on iOS devices (iPhone and iPad).

Important iOS Limitation:

Unlike the Android version, EZer for iOS does NOT have automatic SMS parsing or notification reading capabilities. This is an iOS platform restriction for user privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Same as Android version - account information, manually entered financial data, and receipt images.

Important: We NEVER ask for or store:

  • Bank account passwords or login credentials
  • Credit/debit card numbers, CVV/PIN codes
  • Social Security Numbers or Government ID numbers

2. How We Store Your Information

2.1 Local Storage (iOS Device)

  • Encrypted Database: All financial data stored in encrypted SQLCipher database
  • AES-256 encryption standard
  • Encryption keys stored in iOS Keychain (never leave your device)
  • Data protected by Secure Enclave
  • Cannot be accessed without Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode

3. iOS-Specific Permissions

Permission Purpose Required?
Camera Scan receipts Optional
Face ID / Touch ID Secure app lock Optional
Internet Access Cloud backup, updates Required

Notable Differences from Android:

  • ❌ No SMS access (iOS does not allow third-party apps to read SMS)
  • ❌ No notification listener (iOS platform limitation)
  • ✅ All transactions must be entered manually or imported
  • ✅ More privacy-focused by design due to iOS limitations

4. Data Entry Methods (iOS)

Since iOS does not allow automatic SMS parsing, you have these options:

  • Manual entry: Add transactions using quick-add buttons
  • Bank statement import: Import PDF, CSV, or Excel files
  • Receipt scanning: Take photos for automatic OCR extraction

5. iOS-Specific Privacy Features

  • App Privacy Report: View EZer's data access in iOS Settings
  • Privacy Labels: See our privacy nutrition label on App Store
  • No Tracking: We don't track you across apps or websites

6. Contact Us

Email: privacy@ezerapp.com

Support: support@ezerapp.com

Website: https://www.ezerapp.com/privacy

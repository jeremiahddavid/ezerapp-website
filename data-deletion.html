Data Retention Policy

Local Data (On Your Device):

All data is stored locally on your Android device. When you delete data in-app or uninstall EZer, all local data is immediately removed from your device.

Cloud Backups (Google Drive):

Cloud backups are stored in your personal Google Drive account, not on our servers. You have full control over these backups and can delete them anytime from Google Drive.

Analytics Data:

If you opted into analytics, we collect anonymized, aggregated usage data. This data does NOT include:

  • ‚ùå Your financial transactions
  • ‚ùå Personal information
  • ‚ùå SMS content
  • ‚ùå Any identifiable data

Analytics data is automatically deleted after 90 days.

Legal Retention:

Such retention is limited to the minimum required by law.

GDPR Rights (For EU Users)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will deleting data in-app also delete my cloud backups?

A: No. Cloud backups are stored in your Google Drive and must be deleted separately from Google Drive.

Q: Can I recover my data after deletion?

A: No. Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have cloud backups and haven't deleted them, you can restore from those backups.

Q: What happens if I just uninstall the app?

A: Uninstalling removes all local data from your device. Cloud backups remain in your Google Drive. If you reinstall EZer and sign in with the same Google account, you can restore from cloud backups.

Q: Does EZer keep any data on its servers after I delete my account?

A: EZer is a local-first app. We don't store your financial data on our servers. All your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive (if you enabled cloud backup).

Q: How do I export my data before deleting?

A: Before deleting, go to Settings ‚Üí Data & Export. Export your data in CSV, JSON, Excel, or PDF format. Save the exported files to your device.
